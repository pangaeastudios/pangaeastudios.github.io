By Brandon McKinney
The
PANGAEA
mindset.
The name "Pangaea/Pangea" is derived from Ancient Greek "Pan" ("all, entire, whole") and Gaia ("Mother Earth, land"). This name resembles the holistic nature of my approach to media, design, and creativity; as well as my conscious acknowledgement of human origins.

Featured Projects

Merdeka

by Brandon McKinney - 2019

Mixtape

During the summer of 2019, BR&ON spent most of his time in isolation creating a series of songs that he would later compile into his first musical project & mixtape, 'Merdeka'. The album is an homage to BR&ON's Indonesian upbringing and heritage, as well as a social commentary on the socio-political struggles the country was facing in late 2019.

Out of Breath

by Brandon McKinney, Maaike Maria, Tunga Troll

Single (Official Release)

During the Coronavirus crisis of 2020, BR&ON released his first official single on Spotify, Out of Breath. Made under quarantine on the isolated island of Terschelling, the track expresses themes of anxiety and social commentary that describe the worldwide pandemic that affected everyone at the time.
